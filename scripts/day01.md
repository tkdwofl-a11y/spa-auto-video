# Day 1 - SPA 33 / TOEIC 600 수준

- 총 러닝타임: 20분
- 구성: Part 1~5, 각 4분씩 균등 배분
- 문장 스타일: 쉬운 어휘, 짧고 단순한 문장 구조

## 진행 방식 (공통)

1. 화면에 질문 표시 + 질문 읽어주기
2. "Think Time" 약 10~15초 (시청자가 먼저 답변 연습)
3. 모범답안 읽어주기
4. 다음 질문으로 이동

---

## Part 1. Personal Questions (00:00 - 04:00)

**Q1. Can you tell me about yourself?**
> Hi, my name is [Name]. I work at Hyundai as a [Job Title]. I have been working here for [X] years. In my free time, I like to watch movies and exercise. I'm excited to talk with you today.

**Q2. What do you usually do after work?**
> After work, I usually go home and have dinner with my family. Sometimes I go to the gym to exercise. On weekends, I like to relax and watch TV.

**Q3. Do you like your job? Why or why not?**
> Yes, I like my job. I work with good people, and my work is interesting. Sometimes it is stressful, but I feel proud when I finish a project.

---

## Part 2. Opinion Questions (04:00 - 08:00)

**Q1. Do you think it is better to work in a team or alone?**
> I think it is better to work in a team. When we work together, we can share ideas and solve problems faster. For example, in my company, we often have team meetings to discuss projects. So, teamwork is very important.

**Q2. Do you prefer to work from home or at the office?**
> I prefer to work at the office. I can talk to my coworkers easily and ask questions right away. Also, it is easier to focus at the office because there are fewer distractions.

**Q3. Is it important for a company to care about the environment?**
> Yes, I think it is very important. Many customers care about the environment now. If a company protects the environment, customers will trust it more. So, it is good for business too.

---

## Part 3. Describe a Picture (08:00 - 12:00)

**[화면 안내: 사무실 회의실에서 4명이 노트북과 서류를 보며 회의하는 사진 삽입]**

> In this picture, I can see four people in a meeting room. They are sitting around a table. Two people are looking at a laptop, and one person is holding some documents. I think they are having a business meeting. They look busy and focused. Maybe they are discussing a new project. Overall, this picture shows a normal work meeting at an office.

---

## Part 4. Analyze a Graph (12:00 - 16:00)

**[화면 안내: 2020년~2024년 매출 증가 추세를 보여주는 막대그래프 삽입]**

> This graph shows the sales of a company from 2020 to 2024. In 2020, the sales were low, about 100 million dollars. After that, the sales increased every year. In 2024, the sales reached the highest point, about 400 million dollars. I think the sales increased because the company sold more products online. Overall, the graph shows a strong growth trend.

---

## Part 5. Summarize an Article (16:00 - 20:00)

**[화면 안내: 아래 지문을 화면에 표시]**

> More companies are allowing employees to work from home. A recent survey found that 60% of workers want to keep working remotely, even after the pandemic. Companies say remote work can save money on office space. However, some managers worry that employees are less productive at home. Experts say companies need clear rules for remote work to succeed.

**모범 요약:**
> This article is about remote work. Many companies now let employees work from home. A survey shows that 60% of workers want to continue remote work. Companies can save money because they need less office space. But some managers think employees work less at home. So, experts say companies should make clear rules for remote work.
